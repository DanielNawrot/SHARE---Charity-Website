Welcome to: SHARE

A website designed to connect those in need, with those willing to give!

How we work?

We work by connecting people! Looking to donate? Create an account and share with us what you are willing to give. You will eventually be connected with another individual who in need of your help.

Who are we?

We're a small team of hisghschool students looking to make a differnece in our community. Through projects such as these, we strive to help those in need and tackle social issues happening around us.
